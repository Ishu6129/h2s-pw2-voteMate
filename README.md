# 🗳️ VoteMate — India's Smart Election Assistant

> **Empowering every Indian citizen with clear, accessible, and AI-powered election information.**

VoteMate is a premium, multi-lingual digital assistant designed to simplify the complex landscape of Indian elections. Whether you're a first-time voter or a seasoned citizen, VoteMate provides real-time guidance on voter registration, election timelines, and democratic processes in **7 major Indian languages**.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

In the world's largest democracy, information is power. **VoteMate** bridges the gap between official data and citizen understanding by providing a modern, interactive, and inclusive interface for civic engagement.

---

## 📋 Core Features

| Feature | What it delivers |
|---------|------------------|
| 🤖 **Intelligent Chatbot** | Powered by **Google Gemini AI**, providing nuanced answers to complex election queries in your native language. |
| 🔄 **Reliable Knowledge Base** | Instant access to verified information on voter eligibility, document requirements, and ECI resources. |
| 🌐 **True Multi-lingual Support** | Seamless switching between English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, and Gujarati. |
| 🏛️ **Dynamic Election Modes** | Switch between **Lok Sabha** and **State Assembly** views to see data tailored to the specific election type. |
| 📅 **Visual Timeline** | An interactive journey from the announcement of elections to the final declaration of results. |
| 📱 **Universal Accessibility** | A responsive, inclusive design that works perfectly on everything from low-end mobile devices to high-end desktops. |

---

## ✨ Design & Experience

VoteMate isn't just a tool; it's an experience.
- **Premium Aesthetics**: A soft, light-themed UI inspired by the Indian tricolor, featuring glassmorphism and subtle gradients.
- **Interactive Animations**: Powered by **GSAP**, featuring smooth scroll-triggered transitions and 3D card interactions that make civic learning engaging.
- **Privacy-First**: No personal data collection. Secure server-side processing ensures your interaction with AI is private and safe.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

Built with a focus on performance, security, and scalability:
- **Frontend**: Modern Vanilla JS, HTML5, CSS3, GSAP (Animations).
- **Intelligence**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash API.
- **Infrastructure**: Node.js, Express, Docker, Google Cloud Run.
- **Typography**: Inter & Noto Sans Devanagari (Optimized for Indian languages).

---

## 🏛️ Civic Impact

VoteMate is built to support the vision of **"No Voter Left Behind."** By providing clear steps for voter registration (NVSP/Voter Helpline) and explaining the roles of elected representatives, we help strengthen the foundations of Indian democracy.
